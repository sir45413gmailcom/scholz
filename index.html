About Us

Welcome to KIWOLC Daycare Kindergarten and Lower Primary, a place where children learn, play, and grow. We are a licensed and accredited preschool and kindergarten that offers high-quality education and care for children aged 4months to 6 years old. At KIWOLC Kindergarten, we believe that every child is unique and has the potential to achieve their goals. We follow a child-centered and play-based curriculum that is aligned with the national standards and best practices in early childhood education. Our curriculum covers various domains such as language and literacy, math, science, arts, music, and physical education. We also incorporate multicultural and global perspectives to help children appreciate diversity and become responsible citizens of the world. Our teachers are qualified, experienced, and passionate about working with young children. They use a variety of teaching methods and strategies to meet the individual needs and interests of each child. They also collaborate with parents and families to support the learning and development of their children at home and at school.
